About Surxondaryo

Surxondaryo Region (Uzbek: Surxondaryo viloyati, Сурхондарё вилояти, سرخان‌دریا ولایت, Russian: Сурхандарьинская область, Persian: سرخان‌دریا, UniPers: "sorxāndaryā"), old spelling Surkhandarya Region is a region (viloyat) of Uzbekistan, located in the extreme south-east of the country. Established on March 6, 1941, it borders on Qashqadaryo Region internally, and Turkmenistan,
