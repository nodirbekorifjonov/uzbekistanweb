About Toshkent

Tashkent (/tæʃˈkɛnt/, Uzbek: Toshkent, Тошкент/تاشکند, US also /tɑːʃ-/) (from Russian: Ташкент), or Toshkent (/tɒʃˈkɛnt/; IPA: [tɒʃˈkent]), also historically known as Chach is the capital and largest city of Uzbekistan, as well as the most populous city in Central Asia, with a population of 2,909,000 (2022).[1] It is in northeastern Uzbekistan, near the border with Kazakhstan. Tashkent comes from the Turkic tash and kent, literally translated as "Stone City" or "City of Stones".
